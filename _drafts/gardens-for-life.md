Permaculture was founded in 1968 by Bill Mollison and David Holmgren.&nbsp; It's appropriate to be talking about permaculture since we just lost the father of permacultre just last month.&nbsp; At the age of 88....&nbsp; Permaculture has 3 core values: care for people, care for the planet, and return the surplus.&nbsp; These ideas are ones for our everyday lives.&nbsp;
